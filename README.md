# Liberty Interactive (liberty-interactive)

Liberty Interactive Corporation owned and operated a broad range of video and online commerce companies, including QVC, HSN, and zulily, before being reorganized into Qurate Retail Group in 2018. The corporation tracked separate operating units through Liberty Interactive Group and Liberty Ventures Group tracking stocks.
